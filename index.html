About

One person. Many worlds. A single thread running through all of them.

Samman Shrestha is a final-year BIT student in Kathmandu, working as a QA Engineer by day, a musician learning Newari Baja by evening, and a civic organizer year-round. Across Leo Club, Rotaract, Interact, and the Red Cross, the same instinct shows up again and again: build things that bring people together in person, not just online.

That instinct is now taking shape as LokaLink — a hyperlocal platform built on the idea that community happens at proximity, not at scale.
